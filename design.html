The real problem

You studied English for years. So why does speaking still feel hard?

It's not about grammar rules or vocabulary lists, and it's not about you. Most learners simply never had enough real chances to actually use English out loud — with real people, in real, unscripted moments.

Why English Homestay

If you want to learn to swim, would you only read a book about it?

Or would you get in the water? English works the same way. We're not trying to be another school that teaches you about the language — we're built to get you speaking it, every single day, until it stops feeling like a subject.
